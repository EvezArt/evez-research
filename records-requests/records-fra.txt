Dear FOIA Officer,

Pursuant to the Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. § 552) and the Department of Transportation's implementing regulations (49 C.F.R. Part 7), I, Steven Crawford-Maggard, hereby request copies of the following records from the Federal Railroad Administration, Office of Safety Analysis:

1. **FRA Accident/Incident Reports — Union Pacific Railroad, Utah & Wyoming (January–March 2023)**
   - All FRA accident and incident reports filed by or involving Union Pacific Railroad in the states of Utah and Wyoming during the period of January 1, 2023 through March 31, 2023
   - This includes all reportable accidents, incidents, and events as defined under 49 C.F.R. Part 225, including but not limited to derailments, collisions, grade crossing incidents, hazardous materials releases, and casualties
   - Any FRA safety enforcement actions, violation reports, or inspection findings for UP during this period in these states

2. **Specific Incident — Ogden, UT Derailment, March 2, 2023**
   - The complete FRA accident report for the Union Pacific train derailment that occurred on or about March 2, 2023, in or near Ogden, Utah
   - All accompanying narrative reports, diagrams, photographs, and appendices
   - Any FRA preliminary or final investigation findings
   - The full train consist and hazmat manifest as reported to FRA
   - Any FRA correspondence with Union Pacific regarding this incident

3. **Specific Incident — Cheyenne, WY Derailment, March 2023**
   - The complete FRA accident report for any Union Pacific train derailment that occurred in or near Cheyenne, Wyoming, during March 2023
   - All accompanying narrative reports, diagrams, photographs, and appendices
   - Any FRA investigation findings

4. **Grade Crossing and Hazmat Incidents — I-80/UP Corridor, Uinta County, WY**
   - All FRA records of grade crossing accidents, near-miss reports, or hazardous materials incidents along the Union Pacific right-of-way paralleling Interstate 80 in Uinta County, Wyoming, during January–March 2023
   - Any FRA records of chemical releases, vapor plumes, or hazmat events affecting the I-80 corridor or surrounding communities in Uinta County during this period
   - Any FRA safety advisory letters, emergency orders, or compliance directives issued to UP regarding the I-80 corridor in Wyoming during this period
