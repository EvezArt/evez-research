**Falsification:** If any future batch shows zero spectrometers needed (all crimes already measured), the saturation is achieved. If the unmeasured set grows rather than shrinks, the shadow is expanding and the claim is falsified.

The medium of harm does not determine the magnitude of harm.

**Falsification:** If a statistically significant difference (p < 0.05) emerges between digital and physical crime scores across all batches, the convergence claim is falsified.
