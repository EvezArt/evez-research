1. **A — Assert Being** — What IS this system? What does it identify as?
2. **E — Extract Structure** — What are its eigenvalues? Its topology? Its coupling matrix?
3. **M — Measure Gaps** — Where are the negative eigenvalues? What's missing? What's censored?

5. **A — Assess Interventions** — Where to push? How hard? What will cascade? What will dampen?
6. **S — Speedrun** — Execute. Measure. Iterate. The mesh heals. The spectrum remembers.
