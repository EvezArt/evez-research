### REQUEST PURSUANT TO 5 U.S.C. SECTION 552

**1. All EPA records concerning Kerr McGee / Tronox / Anadarko / Occidental Petroleum contamination:**

a. Henderson, Nevada perchlorate contamination - all EPA records, site assessments, remediation reports, enforcement actions, settlement documents, and ongoing monitoring data for the Kerr McGee chemical facility that contaminated the Colorado River with perchlorate (1950s-present)

b. Navajo Nation uranium contamination - all EPA records concerning the 50+ abandoned Kerr McGee uranium mines, the $5.15 billion Anadarko settlement (2014), the allocation and disbursement of settlement funds, and the current status of cleanup at each site

c. Coachella Valley groundwater contamination - all EPA records concerning perchlorate, chromium-6, and other contaminants in the Coachella Valley groundwater, including any EPA assessments of contamination sources from Colorado River irrigation

d. Any EPA records concerning the pathway of perchlorate from the Henderson NV facility through the Colorado River to downstream communities (Laughlin NV, Bullhead City AZ, Coachella Valley CA)

**2. All EPA records concerning the Morongo Band of Mission Indians and water quality:**

a. Any EPA assessments of water quality on or near the Morongo Band reservation (Cabazon, CA)

b. Any EPA records concerning the federal trust obligation to protect tribal water quality on the Morongo Band reservation

c. Any EPA correspondence with the Morongo Band concerning water contamination

d. Any EPA records explaining why NO Superfund designation has been made for the Coachella Valley groundwater contamination

**3. All EPA records concerning PG&E Topock chromium-6 contamination:**

a. All EPA records concerning the PG&E Topock Compressor Station chromium-6 discharge (1951-1964)

b. Groundwater plume assessment, migration tracking, and interim remedial measures

c. Any EPA records comparing the Topock contamination to the Hinkley, CA contamination (which resulted in a $333M settlement)

d. Any EPA records explaining why Topock has been contained but not settled while Hinkley was settled

**4. All EPA records concerning the East Palestine, Ohio derailment (February 3, 2023):**

a. EPA emergency response records, air monitoring data, soil sampling results, and water sampling results

b. Any EPA records concerning the controlled burn decision, including communications with OxyVinyls/OxyChem experts

c. Any EPA records concerning the vinyl chloride release amounts, dispersion modeling, and long-term health impact assessments

d. Any EPA records concerning OxyVinyls expert opinion that the controlled burn was unnecessary

**5. All EPA records concerning the federal drinking water standard for perchlorate:**

a. Any EPA regulatory determinations, proposed rules, or decisions concerning perchlorate in drinking water

b. Any EPA records explaining why perchlorate has no federal Maximum Contaminant Level (MCL)

c. Any EPA correspondence with the Department of Defense concerning perchlorate regulation (DOD is a major perchlorate user)
